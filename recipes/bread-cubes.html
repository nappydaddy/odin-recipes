Bread Cubes

Description

If you've never squished soft bread into a dough-y cube before eating then you're missing out! Try this recipe if you're ever in the mood for bread but don't want to eat it the same old boring way.

Ingredients

  • Slice of bread (Publix White Mountain Bread works best; you'll see why)

Steps

  1. Remove crust from slice of bread. Set aside for later use.
  2. Tear inner bread into several even pieces.
  3. Take each piece and smush it between your fingers until it forms a perfect cube.
  4. While taking a break from all the smushing, use the flour-dusted crust to give yourself a cocaine-stach. Now you're looking great.
  5. Finish smushing all remaining bread pieces.
  6. Enjoy while looking like a drug addict! The cubes can be eaten whole or in small bites! Delish!
